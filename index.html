Features

Color Grading

Color grading is the process of altering and enhancing the color of a motion picture, video image, or still image electronically, photo-chemically or digitally. Color grading encompasses both color correction and the generation of artistic color effects.

Video Transitions

The way in which any two video shots are joined together is called the transition. In camera transitions include methods such as a pan from one person to another, or a zoom from a mid-shot to a close-up. Post Production transitions include cuts, crossfades, wipes, etc.

Font Recognition

A font is a graphical representation of text that may include a different typeface, point size, weight, color, or design.
